A Timelapse Doodle

A little doodle inspired by Raster's creations with Timelapse Bot and the 12/18/2010 Distributed Hacker Maker Network meeting where I made an impromptu claymation from stills taken of the meeting.

While drawing has never really been an interest of mine, drawing in-real-life frames for animation is pretty fun.

Used in the making:

  • Canon Powershot A550 (nothing fancy)
  • Whiteboard/dry erase marker
  • The free Windows Movie Maker for composition
  • Fedora 14 Linux and ffmpeg2theora for the Ogg/Theora version of the movie.
