Milwaukee Makerspace Grand Opening Road Trip – Preamble

Just as we did in January 2011 for MadHackerHaus 1 in Madison, WI the Distributed Hacker/Maker Network will be taking a road trip – this time to Milwaukee Makerspace’s Grand Opening and Mini Maker Fair! Childrens Powerwheels retrofitted with grown-up sized wheelchair battery/motors and raced? Circuit bending music jam? Intro to Soldering class? Arduino? Biodiesel? DIY electric cars!? If any of this sounds interesting, we’ll see you in Milwaukee on Saturday 4/9/2011 from 12:00noon-10:00pm.
